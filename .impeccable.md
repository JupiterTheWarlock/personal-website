## Design Context

### Users
Visitors discovering JupiterTheWarlock's digital presence — potential collaborators, game players, open-source community members, and tech-curious readers. They arrive from GitHub, social links, or direct search and want a quick sense of who this person is and where to find their work.

### Brand Personality
Retro-terminal, technical, handmade. The ASCII terminal aesthetic is the identity — not decoration layered on top. Three words: **terminal-native, curious, crafted**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark CRT terminal aesthetic with warm amber/orange accents (#DA7756). Monospace typography throughout. Three.js ASCII background adds depth. Anti-reference: generic portfolio templates with gradient hero sections.

### Design Principles
1. Terminal authenticity — every element should feel like it belongs in a terminal session
2. Content over chrome — the ASCII background and glow effects support content, never compete
3. Bilingual parity — zh-CN and en-US must feel equally native
4. Progressive disclosure — nav links to external sites; page sections reveal depth as you scroll
5. Consensus-driven — all personal info sourced from the consensus skill data file
